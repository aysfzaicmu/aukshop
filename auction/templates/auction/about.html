Aukshop is a site where you can buy and sell items. When you sell an item, you have the option of 'Buy it Now', 'Bid', or both 'Buy it Now' and 'Bid'.
You can go to you profile page to view you inventory, see what you have recently bought, and see what you have recently sold.
If you are on the inventory page, you can edit items you have already uploaded.
